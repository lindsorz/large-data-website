Bioluminescence isn’t something made up in James Cameron’s Avatar; it does exist on Earth today. There are lots of living creatures that produce bioluminescence. What is bioluminescence? It literally means light emitted from a living organism; bio, Latin for life/living matter and lumin, Latin for light.
